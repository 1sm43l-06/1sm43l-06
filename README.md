### 🌐 Portfolio: Personal Portfolio (Deployed)

An **experimental project** designed to explore the intersection of **modern web development** and **AI assistance**. Built with **Astro** for performance and **Three.js** for immersive graphics, this portfolio is the result of pushing my own technical boundaries. My objective was to orchestrate the use of **Large Language Models (LLMs)** to transform abstract design concepts—such as **Glassmorphism** and **3D environments**—into functional code, demonstrating **adaptability** and **continuous learning**.

### 🍳 PantryChef: Your smart kitchen manager (Deployed and Published)

PantryChef is your new best friend if you don't know what to cook, hate wasting food, or have loose items in your pantry that you don't know how to use. This website is a recipe book where you can save your recipes, download them as PDFs, manage your kitchen inventory, and even ask AI when you run out of cooking ideas.
